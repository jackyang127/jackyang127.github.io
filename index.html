Hi. I recently moved from the lovely city of Sammamish, Washington to Berkeley, where I'm currently in my second year studying Computer Science at the University of California. Outside of school, I enjoy playing intramural frisbee as well as rooting for the Seattle Seahawks and the Chicago Bulls. I also love nature and you can often find me on hikes and long walks.

Last Summer I had the opportunity to work with some wonderful software engineers at the Jet Propulsion Laboratories in Pasadena, California. More specifically, I was an intern working with other Mission Planning Engineers to write software that would help plan the trajectory and mission sequencing of the Europa Spacecraft. This spacecraft will be launched in 2022 and will conduct many flybys of Europa, a moon of Jupiter. The Europa spacecraft will make many scientific observations of this moon from afar and will eventually give scientists insight into whether or not Europa harbors life.

Next Summer, I will be in Menlo Park working at Facebook as an intern. I don't know exactly what I will be working on yet because we find out our assignments much later, but I'm very excited :)
